MD7 Hashing Factory — Write-up & Exploit

Goal

Submit 100 distinct digit pairs "(A, B)" such that "md5(generateHash(A)) == md5(generateHash(B))" to get the flag.

Vulnerability

"generateHash" reverses digits, maps "(d+1)%10", then converts to number ("+T").
Numeric coercion drops leading zeros, so ""1"" → ""2"" and ""19"" → ""02"" → "2".
Two different strings collide after coercion.

Exploit idea

Pick "A" values avoiding duplicates and digits ending in "'9'".
Let "B = A + "9"".
Each pair collides numerically; submit 100 pairs to get the flag.
